battery_current_privacy_policy

BatteryCurrent is a local battery monitoring utility. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with Analog Wings or any third party. BatteryCurrent stores battery-related measurements locally on the device, including battery current, voltage, temperature, battery percentage, charge/energy history, capacity estimates, calibration results, and user preferences such as display settings and optional rated battery capacity. This information remains in the app’s private storage unless the user manually exports or copies it.
